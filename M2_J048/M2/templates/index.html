How and why to keep track of daily activities and habits

Benefits of tracking activities

If you often feel like you don’t have enough time for all your daily tasks, consider keeping track of what you do during work hours.

Plan your day from anywhere. Access everything you need to do for the day in My Tasks.

Features

You’ll gain control of your time 5

By keeping your daily diary of activities, you’ll manage your time much better. Thus, you’ll do what you enjoy doing.

You’ll be more realistic and organized

you’ll be able to make rational goals and plans for the future.

You’ll make better time estimates

when people make forecasts about their activities, they focus on the specific case from the past and its details.

You’ll be more accountable and productive

By tracking daily activities, we are able to identify what tasks are absorbing the majority of our time. With the data in hand, we can then make the necessary changes.
